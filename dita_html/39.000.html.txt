39.000 Scope of part.

This part prescribes acquisition policies and procedures
for use in acquiring—

(a) Information
technology, including financial management systems, consistent with
other parts of this regulation, OMB Circular No.A-127, Financial
Management Systems and OMB Circular No.A-130, Management of Federal
Information Resources.

(b) Information and communication technology (see 
                     2.101).

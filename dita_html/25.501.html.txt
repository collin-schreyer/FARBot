25.501 General.

The contracting officer-

(a)
						            Must apply the evaluation procedures of this subpart to each line item of an offer unless either the offer or the solicitation specifies evaluation on a group basis (see 25.503);

(b)
						            May rely on the offeror’s certification of end product origin when evaluating a foreign offer;

(c) Must identify and reject
                  offers of end products that are prohibited in accordance with 
                     subpart  25.7;
                  and

(d)
                  When trade agreements are
                     involved,
                     must
                  not use the Buy American statute evaluation factors prescribed in this subpart to
                  provide a preference for one foreign offer over another foreign offer.

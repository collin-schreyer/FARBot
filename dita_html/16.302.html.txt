16.302 Cost contracts.


(a)
Description. A cost contract is a cost-reimbursement contract in which the contractor receives no fee.

(b)
Application. A cost contract may be appropriate for research and development work, particularly with nonprofit educational institutions or other nonprofit organizations.

(c)
Limitations. See 16.301-3.

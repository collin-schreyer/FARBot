16.205-1 Description.

A fixed-price contract with prospective price redetermination provides for-

(a)
                  
						            A firm fixed price for an initial period of contract deliveries or performance; and

(b)
                  
						            Prospective redetermination, at a stated time or times during performance, of the price for subsequent periods of performance.

22.000 Scope of part.

This part-

(a)
						            Deals with general policies regarding contractor labor relations as they pertain to the acquisition process;

(b)
						            Prescribes contracting policy and procedures for implementing pertinent labor laws; and

(c)
						            Prescribes contract clauses with respect to each pertinent labor law.

Parent topic: Part 22 - Application of Labor Laws to Government Acquisitions

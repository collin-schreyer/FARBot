25.000 Scope of part.


25.000 Scope of part.


(a)
						            This part provides policies and procedures for-

(1)
								                Acquisition of foreign supplies, services, and construction materials; and

(2)
								                Contracts performed outside the United States.

(b)
						            It implements 41 U.S.C. chapter 83, Buy American; trade agreements; and other laws and regulations.

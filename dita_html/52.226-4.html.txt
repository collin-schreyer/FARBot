52.226-4 Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area Set-Aside.

As prescribed in 
26.206(b), insert the following clause:
Notice of Disaster or Emergency Area set-Aside (Nov 2007)

(a)
Set-aside area. Offers are solicited only from businesses residing or primarily doing business in ________________________________________________ [Contracting Officer to fill in with definite geographic boundaries.] Offers received from other businesses shall not be considered.

(b)
						            This set-aside is in addition to any small business set-aside contained in this contract.
(End of clause)

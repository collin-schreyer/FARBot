8.700 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes the policies and procedures for implementing–

(a)
41 U.S.C. chapter 85, Committee for Purchase from People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled; and

(b)
                  
						            The rules of the Committee for Purchase from People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled (41 CFR Chapter 51), which implements the AbilityOne program.

Parent topic: Subpart 8.7 - Acquisition from Nonprofit Agencies Employing People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled

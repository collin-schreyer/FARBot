28.103-3 Payment bonds.


(a)
						            A payment bond is required only when a performance bond is required, and if the use of payment bond is in the Government’s interest.

(b)
						            When a contract price is increased, the Government may require additional bond protection in an amount adequate to protect suppliers of labor and material.

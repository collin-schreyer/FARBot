12.103 Commercially available
off-the-shelf (COTS) items.

Commercially available off-the-shelf (COTS)
items are defined in 2.101.
Unless indicated otherwise, all of the policies that apply to commercial products also
apply to COTS items. Section 12.505 lists
the laws that are not applicable to COTS (in addition to 12.503 and 
               12.504).
         

Parent topic: Subpart 12.1 - Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services

53.110 Continuation sheets.

Except as may be otherwise indicated in the FAR, all standard forms prescribed by the FAR may be continued on (a) plain paper of similar specification, or (b) specially constructed continuation sheets (e.g.,

 OF336
). Continuation sheets shall be annotated in the upper right-hand corner with the reference number of the document being continued and the serial page number.

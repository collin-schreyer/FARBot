44.000 Scope of part.


(a) This part prescribes policies
and procedures for consent to subcontracts or advance notification
of subcontracts, and for review, evaluation, and approval of contractors’
purchasing systems.

(b) The consent
and advance notification requirements of subpart  44.2 are
not applicable to prime contracts for commercial supplies or
commercial services acquired pursuant to part  12.

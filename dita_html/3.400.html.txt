3.400 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes policies and procedures that restrict contingent fee arrangements for soliciting or obtaining Government contracts to those permitted by 
               10 U.S.C. 3321(b)(1)
               
             and 41 U.S.C. 3901.

Parent topic: Subpart 3.4 - Contingent Fees

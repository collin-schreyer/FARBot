4.1100 Scope.

This subpart prescribes policies and procedures for requiring contractor registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) to—

(a)
                  
						            Increase visibility of vendor sources (including their geographical locations) for specific supplies and services; and

(b)
                  
						            Establish a common source of vendor data for the Government.

Parent topic: Subpart 4.11 - System for Award Management

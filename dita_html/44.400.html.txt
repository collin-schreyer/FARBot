44.400 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes the policies limiting the contract
clauses a contractor may be required to apply to any subcontractors that
are furnishing commercial
products, including commercial components, or commercial services in
accordance with 41 U.S.C. 3307.

Parent topic: Subpart 44.4 - Subcontracts for Commercial Products and Commercial Services

46.800 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes policies and procedures for limiting contractor liability for loss of or damage to property of the Government that-

(a)
						            Occurs after acceptance and

(b)
						            Results from defects or deficiencies in the supplies delivered or services performed.

Parent topic: Subpart 46.8 - Contractor Liability for Loss of or Damage to Property of the Government

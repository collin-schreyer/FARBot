33.202 Disputes.


41 U.S.C. chapter 71,	Disputes, establishes procedures and requirements for asserting	and resolving claims subject to the Disputes statute. In addition,	the Disputes statute provides for-

(a)
						            The	payment of interest on contractor claims;

(b)
						            Certification	of contractor claims; and

(c)
						            A civil	penalty for contractor claims that are fraudulent or based on a	misrepresentation of fact.

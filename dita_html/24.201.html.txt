24.201 Authority.

The Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C.552, as amended) provides that information is to be made available to the public either by-

(a)
						            Publication in the Federal Register;

(b)
						            Providing an opportunity to read and copy records at convenient locations; or

(c)
						            Upon request, providing a copy of a reasonably described record.

Parent topic: Subpart 24.2 - Freedom of Information Act

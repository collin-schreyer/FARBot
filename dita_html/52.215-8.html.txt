52.215-8 Order of Precedence-Uniform Contract Format.

As prescribed in 
15.209(h), insert the following clause:
Order of Precedence-uniform Contract Format (Oct 1997)

Any inconsistency in this solicitation or contract shall be resolved by giving precedence in the following order:

(a)
						            The Schedule (excluding the specifications).

(b)
						            Representations and other instructions.

(c)
						            Contract clauses.

(d)
						            Other documents, exhibits, and attachments.

(e)
						            The specifications.
(End of clause)

15.200 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes policies and procedures for-

(a)
                  
						            Exchanging information with industry prior to receipt of proposals;

(b)
                  
						            Preparing and issuing requests for proposals (RFPs) and requests for information (RFIs); and

(c)
                  
						            Receiving proposals and information.

Parent topic: Subpart 15.2 - Solicitation and Receipt of Proposals and Information

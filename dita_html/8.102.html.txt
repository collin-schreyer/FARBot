8.102 Policy.

When practicable, agencies shall use excess
personal property as the first source of supply for agency and cost-reimbursement contractor
requirements. Agency personnel shall make
positive efforts to satisfy agency requirements by obtaining and
using excess personal property (including that suitable for adaptation
or substitution) before initiating a contract action.

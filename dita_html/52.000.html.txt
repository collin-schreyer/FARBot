52.000 Scope of part.

This part-

(a)
						            Gives instructions for using provisions and clauses in solicitations and/or contracts;

(b)
						            Sets forth the solicitation provisions and contract clauses prescribed by this regulation; and

(c)
						            Presents a matrix listing the FAR provisions and clauses applicable to each principal contract type and/or purpose (e.g., fixed-price supply, cost-reimbursement research and development).

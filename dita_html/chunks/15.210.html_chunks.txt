Heading: 15.210 Forms.
Text: (a) (a)
Standard Form 33, Solicitation, Offer and Award, and Optional Form 308, Solicitation and Offer-Negotiated Acquisition, may be used to issue RFPs and RFIs.
(b) (b)
Standard Form 30, Amendment of Solicitation/ Modification of Contract, and Optional Form 309, Amendment of Solicitation, may be used to amend solicitations of negotiated contracts.
(c) (c)
Optional Form 17, Offer Label, may be furnished with each request for proposal.

Heading: 32.1001 Policy.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Performance-based payments are the preferred Government financing method when the contracting officer finds them practical, and the contractor agrees to their use.
(b) (b)
						            Performance-based payments are contract financing payments that are not payment for accepted items.
(c) (c)
						            Performance-based payments are fully recoverable, in the same manner as progress payments, in the event of default.
(d) (d)
						            Performance-based payments are contract financing payments and, therefore, are not subject to the interest-penalty provisions of prompt payment (see subpart  32.9). These payments shall be made in accordance with agency policy.

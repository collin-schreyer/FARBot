Heading: 12.603 
         Streamlined solicitation
for commercial products or commercial services.
Text: (a) (a) When a written solicitation will
be issued, the contracting officer may use the following procedure
to reduce the time required to solicit and award contracts for the
acquisition of commercial
products or commercial services. This procedure combines the
synopsis required by 5.203 and
the issuance of the solicitation into a single document.
(b) (b) When using
the combined synopsis/solicitation procedure, the SF1449 is not used
for issuing the solicitation.

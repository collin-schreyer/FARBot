Heading: 35.017-7 
         Limitation on the creation of new FFRDC’s.
Text: (a) (a)
                  The head of the agency submits to Congress a report with respect to such center that describes the purpose, mission, and general scope of effort of the center; and
(b) (b)
                  A period of 60 days, beginning on the date such report is received by Congress, has elapsed.

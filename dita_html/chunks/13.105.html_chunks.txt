Heading: 13.105 Synopsis and posting
requirements.
Text: (a) (a) The contracting officer must comply
with the public display and synopsis requirements of 5.101 and 5.203 unless an exception
in 5.202 applies.
(b) (b) When acquiring commercial products or
commercial services, or supplies or services procured in accordance
with 12.102(f)(1), the contracting
officer may use a combined synopsis and solicitation. In these cases,
a separate solicitation is not required. The contracting officer
must include enough information to permit suppliers to develop quotations
or offers.
(c) (c) See 5.102(a)(6) for the requirement
to post a brand name justification or documentation required by 13.106-1(b) or 13.501.

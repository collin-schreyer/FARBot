Heading: 37.103 Contracting officer
responsibility.
Text: (a) (a) The contracting officer is responsible
for ensuring that a proposed contract for services is proper. For
this purpose the contracting officer shall-
(b) (b) Nonpersonal
services contracts are exempt from the requirements of paragraph
(a)(3) of this section.
(c) (c) Ensure
that performance-based acquisition methods are used to the maximum
extent practicable when acquiring services.
(d) (d) Ensure
that contracts for child care services include requirements for
criminal history background checks on employees who will perform
child care services under the contract in accordance with 
                     34 U.S.C. 20351
                     
                   and
agency procedures.
(e) (e) Ensure
that service contractor reporting requirements are met in accordance
with subpart  4.17, Service
Contracts Inventory.

Heading: 52.219-13 Notice of Set-Aside of Orders.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The Contracting Officer may set aside orders for the small business concerns identified in 19.000(a)(3).
(b) (b)
						            The Contracting Officer will give notice of the order or orders, if any, to be set aside for small business concerns identified in 19.000(a)(3) and the applicable small business program. This notice, and its restrictions, will apply only to the specific orders that have been set aside for any of the small business concerns identified in 19.000(a)(3).

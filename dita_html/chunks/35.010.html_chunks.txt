Heading: 35.010 Scientific and technical reports.
Text: (a) (a)
						            R&D contracts shall require contractors to furnish scientific and technical reports, consistent with the objectives of the effort involved, as a permanent record of the work accomplished under the contract.
(b) (b)
						            Agencies should make R&D contract results available to other Government activities and the private sector.

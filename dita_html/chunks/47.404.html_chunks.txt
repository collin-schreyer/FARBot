Heading: 47.404 Air freight forwarders.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Agencies may use air freight forwarders that are engaged in international air transportation (49 U.S.C. 1301(c)) for U.S. Government-financed movements of property. The rule on disallowance of expenditures in 47.403-3(a) applies also to the air carriers used by these international air freight forwarders.

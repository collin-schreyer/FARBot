Heading: 52.204-5 Women-Owned Business (Other Than Small Business).
Text: (a) (a)
Definition."Women-owned business concern," as used in this provision, means a concern that is at least 51 percent owned by one or more women; or in the case of any publicly owned business, at least 51 percent of its stock is owned by one or more women; and whose management and daily business operations are controlled by one or more women.

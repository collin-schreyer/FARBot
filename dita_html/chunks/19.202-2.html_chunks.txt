Heading: 19.202-2 Locating small business sources.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Before issuing solicitations, make every reasonable effort to find additional small business concerns (see 10.002(b)(2)). This effort should include contacting the agency small business specialist and SBA PCR (or, if a PCR is not assigned, see 19.402(a)).
(b) (b)
						            Publicize solicitations and contract awards through the Governmentwide point of entry (see subparts  5.2 and 5.3).

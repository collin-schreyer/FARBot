Heading: 28.106-2 Substitution of surety bonds.
Text: (a) (a)
						            A new surety bond covering all or part of the obligations on a bond previously approved may be substituted for the original bond if approved by the head of the contracting activity, or as otherwise specified in agency regulation.
(b) (b)
						            When a new surety bond is approved, the contracting officer shall notify the principal and surety of the original bond of the effective date of the new bond.

Heading: 52.232-3 Payments under Personal Services Contracts.
Text: (a) (a) A per diem rate in lieu of subsistence for each day the Contractor is in a travel status away from home or regular place of employment in accordance with Federal Travel Regulations (41 CFR 101-7) as authorized in appropriate Travel Orders; and
(b) (b) Any other transportation expenses if provided for in the Schedule.

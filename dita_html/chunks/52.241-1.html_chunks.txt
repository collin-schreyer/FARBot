Heading: 52.241-1 Electric Service Territory Compliance Representation.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Section 8093 of Public Law100-202 generally requires purchases of electricity by any department, agency, or instrumentality of the United States to be consistent with State law governing the provision of electric utility service, including State utility commission rulings and electric utility franchises or service territories established pursuant to State statute, State regulation, or State-approved territorial agreements.
(b) (b)
						            By signing this offer, the offeror represents that this offer to sell electricity is consistent with Section 8093 of Public Law100-202.
(c) (c)
						            Upon request of the Contracting Officer, the offeror shall submit supporting legal and factual rationale for this representation.

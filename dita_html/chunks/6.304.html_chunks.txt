Heading: 6.304 Approval of the justification.
Text: (a) (a)
                  
						             Except for paragraph (b) of this section, the justification for other than full and open competition shall be approved in writing-
(b) (b)
                  
						             Any justification for a contract awarded under the authority of 6.302-7, regardless of dollar amount, shall be considered approved when the determination required by 6.302-7(c)(1) is made.
(c) (c)
                  
						             A class justification for other than full and open competition shall be approved in writing in accordance with agency procedures. The approval level shall be determined by the estimated total value of the class.
(d) (d)
                  
						             The estimated dollar value of all options shall be included in determining the approval level of a justification.

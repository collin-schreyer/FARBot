Heading: 52.247-39 F.o.b. Inland Point, Country of Importation.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The term "f.o.b. inland point, country of importation," as used in this clause, means free of expense to the Government, on board the indicated type of conveyance of the carrier, delivered to the specified inland point where the consignee’s facility is located.

Heading: 32.113 Customary contract financing.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Financing of shipbuilding, or ship conversion, alteration, or repair, when agency regulations provide for progress payments based on a percentage or stage of completion.
(b) (b)
						            Financing of construction or architect-engineer services purchased under the authority of part  36.
(c) (c)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services awarded under the sealed bid method of procurement in accordance with part  14 through progress payments based on costs in accordance with subpart  32.5.
(d) (d)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services awarded under the competitive negotiation method of procurement in accordance with part  15, through either progress payments based on costs in accordance with subpart  32.5, or performance-based payments in accordance with subpart  32.10 (but not both).
(e) (e)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services awarded under a sole-source acquisition as defined in 2.101 and using the procedures of part  15, through either progress payments based on costs in accordance with subpart  32.5, or performance-based payments in accordance with subpart  32.10 (but not both).
(f) (f)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services through advance payments in accordance with subpart  32.4.
(g) (g)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services through guaranteed loans in accordance with subpart  32.3.
(h) (h)
						            Financing of contracts for supplies or services through any appropriate combination of advance payments, guaranteed loans, and either performance-based payments or progress payments (but not both) in accordance with their respective subparts.

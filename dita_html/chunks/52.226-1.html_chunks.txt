Heading: 52.226-1 Utilization of Indian Organizations and Indian-Owned Economic Enterprises.
Text: (a) (a)
Definitions. As used in this clause:
(b) (b)
						            The Contractor shall use its best efforts to give Indian organizations and Indian-owned economic enterprises (25 U.S.C. 1544) the maximum practicable opportunity to participate in the subcontracts it awards to the fullest extent consistent with efficient performance of its contract.
(c) (c)
						            The Contracting Officer, subject to the terms and conditions of the contract and the availability of funds, will authorize an incentive payment of 5 percent of the amount paid to the subcontractor. The Contracting Officer will seek funding in accordance with agency procedures.

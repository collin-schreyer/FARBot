Heading: 46.402 Government contract quality assurance at source.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Performance at any other place would require uneconomical disassembly or destructive testing;
(b) (b)
						            Considerable loss would result from the manufacture and shipment of unacceptable supplies, or from the delay in making necessary corrections;
(c) (c)
						            Special required instruments, gauges, or facilities are available only at source;
(d) (d)
						            Performance at any other place would destroy or require the replacement of costly special packing and packaging;
(e) (e)
						            Government inspection during contract performance is essential; or
(f) (f)
						            It is determined for other reasons to be in the Government’s interest.

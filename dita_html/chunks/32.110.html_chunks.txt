Heading: 32.110 Payment of subcontractors
under cost-reimbursement prime contracts.
Text: (a) (a) The
payments are made under the criteria in subpart  32.5 for
customary progress payments based on costs, 32.202-1 for commercial product or commercial service purchase
financing, or 32.1003 for performance-based
payments, as applicable.
(b) (b) If customary
progress payments are made, the payments do not exceed the progress
payment rate in 32.501-1,
unless unusual progress payments to the subcontractor have been
approved in accordance with 32.501-2.
(c) (c) If customary
progress payments are made, the subcontractor complies with the
liquidation principles of 32.503-8, 32.503-9, and 32.503-10.
(d) (d) If performance-based
payments are made, the subcontractor complies with the liquidation
principles of 32.1004(d).
(e) (e) The subcontract
contains financing payments terms as prescribed in this part.

Heading: 16.202-2 Application.
Text: (a) (a) There
is adequate price competition;
(b) (b) There are
reasonable price comparisons with prior purchases of the same or
similar supplies or services made on a competitive basis or supported
by valid certified cost or pricing data;
(c) (c) Available
cost or pricing information permits realistic estimates of the probable
costs of performance; or
(d) (d) Performance
uncertainties can be identified and reasonable estimates of their
cost impact can be made, and the contractor is willing to accept
a firm fixed price representing assumption of the risks involved.

Heading: 17.700 Scope of subpart.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Compliance with this subpart is in addition to the policies and procedures for interagency acquisitions set forth in subpart  17.5. This subpart prescribes policies and procedures specific to acquisitions of supplies and services by nondefense agencies on behalf of the Department of Defense (DoD).
(b) (b) This subpart implements Pub. L. 110-181, section 801, as amended (10 U.S.C. 3201 note prec.).

Heading: 52.228-17 
         Individual Surety—Pledge
of Assets (Bid Guarantee).
Text: (a) (a)Offerors
shall obtain from each person acting as an individual surety on
a bid guarantee—
(b) (b)The Offeror
shall include with its offer the information required at paragraph
(a) of this provision within the timeframe specified in the provision
at FAR 52.228-1, Bid Guarantee,
or as otherwise established by the Contracting Officer.
(c) (c)The Contracting
Officer may release the security interest on the individual surety's
assets in support of a bid guarantee based upon evidence that the
offer supported by the individual surety will not result in contract
award.

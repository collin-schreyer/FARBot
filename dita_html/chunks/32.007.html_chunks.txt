Heading: 32.007 Contract financing payments.
Text: (a) (a)
(b) (b)
						            For advance payments, loans, or other arrangements that do not involve recurrent submission of contract financing requests, the designated payment office will make payment in accordance with the applicable contract financing terms or as directed by the contracting officer.
(c) (c)
						            A proper contract financing request must comply with the terms and conditions specified by the contract. The contractor must correct any defects in requests submitted in the manner specified in the contract or as directed by the contracting officer.
(d) (d)
						            The designated billing office and designated payment office must annotate each contract financing request with the date their respective offices received the request.
(e) (e)
						            The Government will not pay an interest penalty to the contractor as a result of delayed contract financing payments.

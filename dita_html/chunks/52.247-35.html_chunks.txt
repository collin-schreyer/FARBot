Heading: 52.247-35 F.o.b. Destination, Within Consignee’s Premises.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The term "f.o.b. destination, within consignee’s premises," as used in this clause, means free of expense to the Government delivered and laid down within the doors of the consignee’s premises, including delivery to specific rooms within a building if so specified.

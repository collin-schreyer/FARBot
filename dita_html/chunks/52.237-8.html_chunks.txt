Heading: 52.237-8 Restriction on Severance Payments to Foreign Nationals.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR), at 31.205-6(g)(6), limits the cost allowability of severance payments to foreign nationals employed under a service contract performed outside the United States unless the agency grants a waiver pursuant to FAR 37.113-1 before contract award.

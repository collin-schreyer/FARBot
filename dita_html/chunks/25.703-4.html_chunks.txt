Heading: 25.703-4 Waiver.
Text: (a) (a)
						            An agency or contractor seeking	a waiver of the requirements of 25.703-2 or 25.703-3, consistent with	section 6(b)(5) of the Iran Sanctions Act or 22 U.S.C. 8551(b),	respectively, and the Presidential Memorandum of September 23, 2010 (75	FR 67025), shall submit the request to the Office of Federal Procurement	Policy, allowing sufficient time for review and approval.
(b) (b)
						            Agencies	may request a waiver on an individual or class basis; however, waivers	are not indefinite and can be cancelled, if warranted.

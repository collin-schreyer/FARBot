Heading: 28.204-1 United States bonds
or notes.
Text: (a) (a) Turn
securities over to the finance or other authorized agency official;
or
(b) (b) Deposit
them with the Treasurer of the United States, a Federal Reserve
Bank (or branch with requisite facilities), or other depository
designated for that purpose by the Secretary of the Treasury, under
procedures prescribed by the agency concerned and Treasury Department
CircularNo.154 (exception: The contracting officer shall deposit
all bonds and notes received in the District of Columbia with the
Treasurer of the United States).

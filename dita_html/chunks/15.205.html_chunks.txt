Heading: 15.205 Issuing solicitations.
Text: (a) (a)
                  
						            The contracting officer shall issue solicitations to potential sources in accordance with the policies and procedures in 5.102, 19.202-4, and part  6.
(b) (b)
                  
						            A master solicitation, as described in 14.203-3, may also be used for negotiated acquisitions.

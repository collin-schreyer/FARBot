Heading: 31.205-30 Patent costs.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The following patent costs are allowable to the extent that they are incurred as requirements of a Government contract (but see 31.205-33):
(b) (b)
						            General counseling services relating to patent matters, such as advice on patent laws, regulations, clauses, and employee agreements, are allowable (but see 31.205-33).
(c) (c)
						            Other than those for general counseling services, patent costs not required by the contract are unallowable. (See also 31.205-37.)

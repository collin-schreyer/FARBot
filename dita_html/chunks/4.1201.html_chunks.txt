Heading: 4.1201 Policy.
Text: (a) (a) Offerors and quoters are required
to complete electronic annual representations and certifications
in SAM accessed via https://www.sam.gov as a part of required
registration (see FAR 4.1102).
(b) (b)
(c) (c) Data in
SAM is archived and is electronically retrievable. Therefore, when
a prospective contractor has completed representations and certifications
electronically in SAM, the contracting officer must reference the
date of SAM verification in the contract file to satisfy contract
file documentation requirements of 4.803(a)(11).
However, if an offeror identifies changes to SAM data pursuant to
the FAR provisions at 52.204-8(d)
or 52.212-3(b), the contracting
officer must include a copy of the changes in the contract file.
(d) (d) The contracting
officer shall incorporate the representations and certifications
by reference in the contract (see 52.204-19, or for acquisitions
of commercial products
or commercial services see 52.212-4(v)).

Heading: 52.247-10 Net Weight-General Freight.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The net weight of the shipment shall be determined by deducting the tare weight of the vehicle (determined by having the empty vehicle with a full tank of fuel weighed by a certified weighmaster on a certified scale) from the gross weight of the vehicle (determined by having the loaded vehicle with a full tank of fuel weighed by a certified weighmaster on a certified scale).
(b) (b)
						            The Contractor shall attach the original copies of the empty and loaded weight certificates to the invoice for services.

Heading: 9.500 Scope of subpart.
Text: (a) (a)
                  
						            Prescribes responsibilities, general rules, and procedures for identifying, evaluating, and resolving organizational conflicts of interest;
(b) (b)
                  
						            Provides examples to assist contracting officers in applying these rules and procedures to individual contracting situations; and
(c) (c)
                  
						            Implements section 8141 of the 1989 Department of Defense Appropriation Act, Pub.L.100-463, 102 Stat.2270-47 (1988).

Heading: 49.204 Deductions.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The agreed price for any part of the termination inventory purchased or retained by the contractor, and the proceeds from any materials sold that have not been paid or credited to the Government;
(b) (b)
						            The fair value, as determined by the TCO, of any part of the termination inventory that, before transfer of title to the Government or to a buyer under part  45, is lost or so damaged as to become undeliverable (normal spoilage is excepted, as is inventory for which the Government has expressly assumed the risk of loss); and
(c) (c)
						            Any other amounts as appropriate in the particular case.

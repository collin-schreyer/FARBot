Heading: 16.603-3 Limitations.
Text: (a) (a)
						            Commit the Government to a definitive contract in excess of the funds available at the time the letter contract is executed;
(b) (b)
						            Be entered into without competition when competition is required by part  6; or
(c) (c)
						            Be amended to satisfy a new requirement unless that requirement is inseparable from the existing letter contract. Any such amendment is subject to the same requirements and limitations as a new letter contract.

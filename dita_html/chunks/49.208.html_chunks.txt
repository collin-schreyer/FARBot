Heading: 49.208 Equitable adjustment	after partial termination.
Text: (a) (a)
						            When	the contracting officer retains responsibility for negotiating the	equitable adjustment and executing a supplemental agreement, the	contracting officer shall ensure that no portion of an increase	in price is included in a termination settlement made or in process.
(b) (b)
						            The TCO	shall also ensure that no portion of the costs included in the equitable	adjustment are included in the termination settlement.

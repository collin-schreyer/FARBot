Heading: 25.201 Policy.
Text: (a) (a)
                  
                  Except as provided in 25.202, use only domestic
construction materials in construction contracts performed in the United
States.

                  restricts the purchase of construction materials that are not domestic
                  construction materials. For manufactured construction materials, the Buy American
                     statute, E.O. 13881, and E.O. 14005
                     use
                  a two-part test to define domestic construction materials.

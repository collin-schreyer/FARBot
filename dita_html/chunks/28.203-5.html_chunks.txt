Heading: 28.203-5 Exclusion of individual
sureties.
Text: (a) (a)An
individual may be excluded from acting as a surety on bonds submitted
by offerors on procurement by the executive branch of the Federal
Government, by the acquiring agency's head or designee utilizing
the procedures in subpart  9.4. The
exclusion shall be for the purpose of protecting the Government.
(b) (b)An
individual may be excluded for any of the following causes:
(c) (c)An
individual surety excluded pursuant to this section shall be entered
as an exclusion in the System for Award Management (see 9.404).
(d) (d)Contracting
officers shall not accept the bonds of individual sureties whose
names appear in an active exclusion record in the System for Award
Management (see 9.404) unless the acquiring
agency's head or a designee states in writing the compelling reasons
justifying acceptance.
(e) (e)An
exclusion of an individual surety under this section will also preclude
such party from acting as a contractor in accordance with subpart  9.4.

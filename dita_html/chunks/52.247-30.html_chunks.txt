Heading: 52.247-30 F.o.b. Origin, Contractor’s Facility.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The term "f.o.b. origin, contractor’s facility," as used in this clause, means free of expense to the Government delivered on board the indicated type of conveyance of the carrier (or of the Government, if specified) at the designated facility, on the named street or highway, in the city, county, and State from which the shipment will be made.

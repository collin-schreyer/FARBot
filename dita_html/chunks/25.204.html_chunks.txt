Heading: 25.204 Evaluating offers of
foreign construction material.
Text: (a) (a)
                   Offerors proposing to use foreign
construction material other than that listed by the Government in
the applicable clause at 52.225-9,
paragraph (b)(2), or 52.225-11,
paragraph (b)(3), or covered by the WTO GPA or a Free Trade Agreement
(paragraph (b)(2) of 52.225-11),
must provide the information required by paragraphs (c) and (d)
of the respective clauses.
(b) (b)
(c) (c)
                   Offerors
also may submit alternate offers based on use of equivalent domestic
construction material to avoid possible rejection of the entire
offer if the Government determines that an exception permitting
use of a particular foreign construction material does not apply.
(d) (d)
                   If the
contracting officer awards a contract to an offeror that proposed
foreign construction material not listed in the applicable clause
in the solicitation (paragraph (b)(2) of 52.225-9, or paragraph
(b)(3) of 52.225-11), the contracting
officer must add the excepted materials to the list in the contract
clause.

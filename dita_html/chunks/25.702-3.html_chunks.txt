Heading: 25.702-3 Remedies.
Text: (a) (a)
						            The contracting officer may terminate the contract;
(b) (b) The suspending and debarring official may suspend the contractor in accordance with the procedures in 
                     subpart  9.4; and
(c) (c)
                  The suspending and debarring may debar the contractor for a period not to exceed 3 years in accordance with the procedures in 
                     subpart  9.4.

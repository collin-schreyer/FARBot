31.205-29 Plant protection costs.

Costs of items such as-

(a)
						            Wages, uniforms, and equipment of personnel engaged in plant protection,

(b)
						            Depreciation on plant protection capital assets, and

(c)
						            Necessary expenses to comply with military requirements, are allowable.

Parent topic: 31.205 Selected costs.

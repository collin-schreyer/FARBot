3.1102 Policy.

The Government’s policy is to require contractors to-

(a)
                  
						            Identify and prevent personal conflicts of interest of their covered employees; and

(b)
                  
						            Prohibit covered employees who have access to nonpublic information by reason of performance on a Government contract from using such information for personal gain.

Parent topic: Subpart 3.11 - Preventing Personal Conflicts of Interest for Contractor Employees Performing Acquisition Functions

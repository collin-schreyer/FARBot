28.105-2 Patent infringement bonds.


(a)
						            Contracts providing for patent indemnity may require these bonds only if-

(1)
								                A performance bond is not furnished; and

(2)
								                The financial responsibility of the contractor is unknown or doubtful.

(b)
						            The contracting officer shall determine the penal sum.

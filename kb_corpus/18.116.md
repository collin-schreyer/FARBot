# FAR 18.116 — Service-disabled Veteran-owned Small Business (SDVOSB) sole source awards

Part 18 - Emergency Acquisitions › Subpart 18.1 - Available Acquisition Flexibilities

Contracts may be awarded to Service-disabled Veteran-owned Small Business (SDVOSB) concerns eligible under the SDVOSB Program on a sole source basis. (See 19.1406 .)

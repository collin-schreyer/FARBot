# FAR 4.1401 — Applicability

Part 4 - Administrative and Information Matters › Subpart 4.14 - Reporting Executive Compensation and First-Tier Subcontract Awards

(a) This subpart applies to all contracts with a value of $30,000 or more. Nothing in this subpart requires the disclosure of classified information. (b) Reporting of subcontract information will be limited to the first-tier subcontractor.
